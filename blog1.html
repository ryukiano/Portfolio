Jobs are getting saturated and automated day by day . I am a SEO developer and currently learning coding. SEO job is pretty much dead now, google’s AI is managing SEO now . You must be wondering how to tackle this. Abhinav chikara ( a top notch designer of our country) adviced to become generalist instead of a specialist. Since we are living in information age, it’s really hard to say a job is going to stay around. So you have to pickup a skill and have to go in length of it , after that go for breadth i.e. across fields related it. I am currently coding and aiming to learn other skills. You should try too so whatever obstacle comes in future you’ll be ready to face it.
